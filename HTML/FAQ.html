What are this website function?

On this website, you will be able to:

  • Find a job offer that suits you, if it exists
  • Create your own job offer
  • Search the history of your jobs and your bids
  • Introduce you to the community of job finder

Find a job offer

How do I find a job?

You can search for a job in the page Find a Job Offer. Once you enter the page and you are logged in, you can filter all the job offer currently available in order to find the perfect one for you!

How does the filter work?

You can filter jobs by payment, date, tipology and tags. Each job is associated with more tags, so by searching with tags you will be able to land on your desired job.

Do I need to login to search a job offer?

You can search a job offer without being logged in, but in order to fully see the job offer information, you will have to be logged in.

Create a job offer

How can I create a job offer?

Once you logged in, you can create a job offer in Create a Job Offer page. Once you enter the page, you will find a form with all the informations necessary to create the job offer. After you created the new offer, you can go to your User Profile and check back later if anyone made a bid on it!

Who can see the job I created?

Everyone that finds it in Find a job offer page.

Where can I see all the offer I created?

All the informations regarding your job offer will be stored in your User Profile. There you will find your personal User Informations and all the informations regarding your offers and your bids.

What happens if nobody bid on my offer?

If nobody bids on your offer, when the timer runs out the job offer will be counted as unsuccesfull. But do not worry, you can just create a new one!

Do I need to login to create an offer or make a bid?

The login is necessary to join and to create any job offer or to bid on any job. We can't let user make offers without being authenticated to the page.

User Profile

What can I do in User Profile?

You can use User Profile to check out your private informations, change them and check out your current and past jobs offer and bids.
